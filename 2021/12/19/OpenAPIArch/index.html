Conclusion

In this article is described the setup in Rach to have dotnet frameworks and setup of a simples OpenAPI service and client console taking advanatge of the OpenAPI specification to generate the class to interact with the service in a Async way.
The good thing is that is relatively quick to setup a consumer using this methodology. The bad part is that by incorporating this abstraction the developer may lack the knowledge to understand what is happening under the hood so is important to support the communities that supply this packages not only for a contribution effort but also to deepdive on the internals.
A good extend for this article would be to setup a docker enviroment to run the service and setup a CI/CD process for it. Also to extend the service or create something more appealing.
